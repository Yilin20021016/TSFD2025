Technical tour to ITRI requests the COVID19 Health Declaration form

Uploaded on 2022.10.28

Dear Colleagues and friends of IC3MT,

 

We hope you are fine.

Below are the notices to those who are going to ITRI.

  1. Please fill out the enclosed ITRI Personal Information and Health Declaration Form, and hand it in when you register at the conference desk.
  2. On the date (Nov. 14), please prepare the following items for investigation at the ITRI entrance.
  3. We appreciate your corporation.

 

Nationality

Documents

Taiwan (ROC)

One of the following proofs

1.      Record of two doses of the COVID-19 vaccine.

2.      PCR negative certificate within three days.

3.      Negative certificate of rapid test on the same day or the day before.

Please take a picture of the negative result in the same frame with a device (such as a mobile phone, computer, etc.) that can display the test date and time and save the picture on your mobile phone as proof.

Foreign

Negative certificate of rapid test on the same day or the day before.

 

Please take a picture of the negative result in the same frame with a device (such as a mobile phone, computer, etc.) that can display the test date and time and save the picture on your mobile phone as proof.

 

Sincerely,

IC3MT2022 Secretariate
