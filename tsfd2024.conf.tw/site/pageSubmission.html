INSTRUCTIONS TO CONTRIBUTORS

All papers must be submitted in English. An abstract of up to 300 words is required for each paper. The abstract should include the title, keywords, name(s) of the author(s), and organization(s). The corresponding author should provide a postal address, telephone/fax number, and email address.

Abstracts should be submitted via the IC3MT website and marked with the relevant themes. Authors of accepted abstracts are required to prepare an Extended Abstract (up to 2 pages) or a Full Paper (limited to 6-8 pages) according to a specified camera-ready format.

The extended abstracts and full papers will be reviewed before acceptance. Full Papers, if desired, will be published in the EI periodicals:

  • Key Engineering Materials (KEM)
  • Materials Science Forum (MSF)
  • Applied Mechanics and Materials (AMM)
  • Advances in Science and Technology (AST)

For more information such as paper format and publication, submitting programs, etc., please see the conference website, or contact the conference secretariats.
