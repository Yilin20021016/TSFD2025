Machining Technology:

Machining of Novel, Difficult-to-cut and Free Machining; Precision, Micro and Nano-Machining; Robotic Engineering; Power system; Power Electronic; High-Speed Machining and Abrasive Machining; Non-conventional Machining; Monitoring of the Machining Process; Surface Integrity and Machining Quality; Green Process/Manufacturing; Cutting/Grinding fluids; New Machine Tools and Tooling Technology; Cutting Mechanisms, Analysis and Simulation; Virtual Manufacturing; Engineering Education.

　

Materials Technology:

New Tool Materials and Abrasive Materials for machining; Advanced Processing, Technologies of Materials; Novel structural materials; Materials Modeling and Simulation; Functional materials; Materials Characterization and Evaluation.

　

Mechanical Technology:

Mechanics of Solids, Structures and Fluids; Vibration, Wave Propagation, Dynamics and Control; Heat Transfer, Thermal, and Fluid Engineering; Energy Engineering; Micro- and Nano-Systems Engineering and Emerging Technologies; CAD/CAM/CAE/RP.
