IC3MT2022 will cancel the sightseeing tour to Yingge/Daxi.

Uploaded on 2022.10.31

Dear Colleagues and friends of IC3MT,

 

We are sorry that we have to cancel the 11/14 tour to Yingge and Daxi because very few people have registered for the tour. And it is impossible to accept new registration during the conference period because of the travel insurance regulation. Those registered for the Yingge and Daxi tour will receive a USD 50 refund at the conference desk. We cordially invite those registered to consider joining the technical tour to ITRI, which is scheduled for 11/14 too. Please reply to our email to make your reservation at ITRI. Thank you for being so understanding.

 

Sincerely,

 

IC3MT2022 Secretariat
