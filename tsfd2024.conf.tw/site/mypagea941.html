Public Trasit

Getting to Main Campus II from Taoyuan Airport

1. Take a bus from the airport to Taipei Railway Station.
2. In order to get to the main campus, get on the MRT at Taipei Main Station and take either the red line (Tamsui-Xinyi Line) or the green line (Songshan-Xindian Line) to Guting Station.
3. Go out exit 5 and walk straight up Heping East Rd. for about 10 minutes.

Getting to Main Campus II from Songshan Airport

1. Take the brown line (Wenhu Line) from Songshan Airport Station to Nanjing Fuxing Station.
2. From there, switch to the green line (Songshan-Xindian Line) to Guting Station.
3. Go out exit 5 and walk straight up Heping East Rd. for about 10 minutes.

　

Driving Directions
